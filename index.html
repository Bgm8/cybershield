By entering your free keys from services like VirusTotal or AbuseIPDB on the settings tab, the CyberShield engine will perform API calls using your personal key thresholds, granting you unlimited daily checks.
Are my API keys safe? ▼
Absolutely. We save your keys inside your browser's local storage only. We never save them on our databases, ensuring total developer privacy.
Can I delete my account scans history? ▼
Yes, logged in users can purge their database logs entirely from their dashboard panel at any time.

📖 About CyberShield

CyberShield was founded on a simple principle: everyone has the right to navigate the internet safely. In an era of rampant phishing, credential breaches, and ransomware distribution, clicking a link or downloading a file should not feel like playing Russian roulette.

We aggregate the security analyses of 91+ independent scanners, spam watchlists, certificate details, and threat pulse reports.

Our Commitments:

  • No Tracking: We never log your personal coordinates, IPs, or location telemetry.
  • No Data Brokering: We don't sell information to ad agencies or data aggregators.
  • Open BYOK Access: Integrating your developer keys allows unlimited free checks forever.

Underlying Intelligence:

Platform integrations include: VirusTotal, AbuseIPDB, AlienVault OTX, HaveIBeenPwned, PhishTank, SSL Labs, Google Safe Browsing, MalwareBazaar, and URLScan.io.
