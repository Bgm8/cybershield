# CyberShield 30-Day Marketing Promotion Calendar
## 30 Minutes Per Day Traffic Acquisition Playbook

This calendar provides a highly structured daily routine targeting developers, small businesses, and general internet users in India to drive organic traffic, build SEO backlinks, and convert users to Pro subscriptions.

---

### Phase 1: Launch and Community Seed (Days 1–10)
**Goal:** Build awareness of the free link threat scanning tools in tech communities.

*   **Day 1:** Share the tool on Indian subreddits: r/india, r/developersIndia, r/techindia. Focus on "I built a free tool to help my parents check suspicious WhatsApp links."
*   **Day 2:** Post on LinkedIn: "Phishing scams in India are at an all-time high. I built CyberShield to protect people from UPI and WhatsApp scams. Try it for free."
*   **Day 3:** Share on Twitter/X using tags: `#CyberSecurity`, `#UPI`, `#ScamAlert`, `#India`.
*   **Day 4:** Post an answer on Quora to: "How do I check if a WhatsApp link is safe?" link to CyberShield.
*   **Day 5:** Pitch to Indian newsletter editors (e.g., *The Ken*, *FactorDaily*, *Inc42*) introducing the UPI Fraud Checker.
*   **Day 6:** Publish a post on dev.to: "Building a Phishing & UPI Fraud Checker with FastAPI and Supabase". Link back to CyberShield.
*   **Day 7:** Reach out to 5 local business groups on Facebook/Telegram. Offer a demo of how their employees can verify links.
*   **Day 8:** Post on Quora: "What are the common UPI scams in India and how can I stay safe?"
*   **Day 9:** Write a short thread on Twitter/X showing a screenshot of a fake TCS recruiting website and how CyberShield caught it.
*   **Day 10:** Update your GitHub repository README with links, screenshots, and call-to-actions to the hosted site.

---

### Phase 2: SEO and Content Reinforcement (Days 11–20)
**Goal:** Build links, optimize keywords, and index the 5 core articles.

*   **Day 11:** Submit your site map to Google Search Console to speed up crawling of the `/blog` index.
*   **Day 12:** Share Article 1 ("How to Check if a WhatsApp Link is Safe") on Medium. Link back to the main site.
*   **Day 13:** Answer Quora queries about "How to spot fake job offers in Bangalore/Delhi." Link to Article 5.
*   **Day 14:** Post a walkthrough on LinkedIn showing how the QR Code scanner prevents payment terminal scams.
*   **Day 15:** Contact 3 security bloggers. Offer to write a free guest post on "UPI Deep Link Security."
*   **Day 16:** Post a carousel on LinkedIn: "5 Signs You are Looking at a Fake Website."
*   **Day 17:** Find active threads on Twitter/X about compromised accounts and offer CyberShield's free password checker as a diagnostic check.
*   **Day 18:** Share Article 3 ("UPI Payment Scams in India") on local Reddit and Hacker News.
*   **Day 19:** Search Google for blogs writing about Indian cybersecurity, email editors offering a brief mention of CyberShield's free scanners.
*   **Day 20:** Record a 60-second video demoing the QR scanner. Upload to YouTube Shorts & Instagram Reels.

---

### Phase 3: Conversions and Business Pitch (Days 21–30)
**Goal:** Direct traffic towards Razorpay Pro subscription conversions and developer API key checkouts.

*   **Day 21:** Publish LinkedIn post highlighting the "Bring Your Own Key" (BYOK) model for tech businesses.
*   **Day 22:** Email 10 small startup founders in your network. Pitch the Pro Plan to audit employee links.
*   **Day 23:** Post on IndieHackers: "How I built a SaaS platform for ₹199/month using FastAPI and Razorpay."
*   **Day 24:** Create a comparison post: "CyberShield vs VirusTotal — Why localized context matters for Indian users."
*   **Day 25:** Share a video of the job checker detecting fake recruitment domains (impersonations) on LinkedIn.
*   **Day 26:** Review click stats on your affiliate banners in the Admin console, adjust card positions for optimal CTR.
*   **Day 27:** Write a thread on Twitter/X detailing the top 5 trending UPI handles used by scammers this week.
*   **Day 28:** Post in Facebook freelancer groups. Pitch the platform as a tool to verify client contract agreements.
*   **Day 29:** Offer a "Free security audit" day on Twitter/X — users reply with links, and you scan and report back using CyberShield.
*   **Day 30:** Review your Google Search Console keyword queries. Adjust header tag keywords on `index.html` to target rising keywords.
